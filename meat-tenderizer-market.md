<p><p>The meat tenderizer market is categorized into two main types: mechanical control and digital control. Digital control, on the other hand, utilizes advanced technology to optimize tenderization processes, often incorporating sensors and programmed settings to achieve consistent results. Both types aim to improve meat quality and tenderness, catering to different consumer preferences and industrial requirements.</p></p>
