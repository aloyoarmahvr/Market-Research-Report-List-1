<p><p>The Closed-Circuit SCBA market comprises two main types of systems: High Pressure Open-Circuit SCBA and Low Pressure Open-Circuit SCBA. High Pressure Open-Circuit SCBA utilizes compressed air at elevated pressures for extended duration and performance, ideal for hazardous environments. In contrast, Low Pressure Open-Circuit SCBA operates at lower pressures and is generally lighter, catering to tasks requiring less intensive air supply. Both systems are designed to protect users in environments with limited or contaminated air, ensuring safety and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
