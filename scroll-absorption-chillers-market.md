<p><p>The Scroll Absorption Chillers market encompasses two main types: Scroll Chillers and Absorption Chillers. Scroll Chillers utilize a refrigerant passing through spirally arranged scrolls to provide efficient cooling with minimal energy consumption. In contrast, Absorption Chillers operate using heat sources, such as natural gas or waste heat, to achieve cooling through a chemical absorption process. Both technologies cater to diverse industrial and commercial applications, offering energy-efficient cooling solutions while contributing to sustainability efforts in various sectors.</p></p>
