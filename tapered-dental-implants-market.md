<p><strong>The Tapered Dental Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Root-form Dental Implants</li><li>Plate-form Dental Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tapered dental implants market includes two main types: root-form and plate-form implants. Root-form implants resemble the shape of natural tooth roots, allowing for easy placement in the bone and providing stability in various bone types, making them widely used in modern dentistry. Plate-form implants, on the other hand, are flat and designed for patients with limited bone width. They require a different surgical approach and are ideal for specific anatomical situations, offering support and retention in narrow ridges.</p></p>
