<p><strong>The Airborne SATCOM Terminals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ka-band</li><li>Ku-band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airborne SATCOM terminals operate primarily in two frequency bands: Ka-band and Ku-band. The Ka-band offers higher data rates and greater bandwidth, making it ideal for high-capacity applications like in-flight connectivity and real-time video streaming. In contrast, the Ku-band provides broader coverage and is often used for broader satellite communications, including voice and data transmission. Both bands cater to differing operational needs, with Ka-band focusing on high-speed applications and Ku-band emphasizing reliability and extensive geographical reach.</p></p>
