<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarms</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarms</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The standalone battery-operated residential smoke alarm market comprises three main types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric alarms detect larger smoke particles from smoldering fires, offering quicker response times for certain types of fires. Ionization smoke alarms are more responsive to fast-flaming fires due to their sensitivity to smaller particles. Combination smoke alarms integrate both technologies, providing comprehensive protection by detecting various fire types, enhancing home safety for residents.</p></p>
