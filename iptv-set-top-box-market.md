<p><p>The IPTV Set-Top Box market encompasses various types including Over The Top (OTT) and Direct Terrestrial Transmission (DTT). OTT refers to content delivered over the internet, bypassing traditional cable or satellite services, providing flexibility and a wide range of streaming options. In contrast, DTT involves receiving digital television signals directly via antennas, enabling access to terrestrial channels without requiring a cable subscription. Both market types cater to diverse consumer preferences, emphasizing convenience and accessibility in the evolving media landscape.</p></p>
