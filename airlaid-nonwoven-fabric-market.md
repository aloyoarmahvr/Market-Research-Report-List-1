<p><p>The airlaid nonwoven fabric market encompasses three main types: latex-bonded, thermal-bonded, and multi-bonded types. Latex-bonded airlaid fabrics utilize latex adhesives to create a durable and flexible material ideal for applications in hygiene and medical products. Thermal-bonded types employ heat to bond fibers, offering increased strength and softness, suitable for filtration and insulation. Multi-bonded fabrics integrate different bonding techniques, enhancing performance traits like absorbency and durability, making them versatile for various industrial uses, including cleaning and personal care items.</p></p>
