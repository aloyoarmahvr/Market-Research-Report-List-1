<p><p>The Human Leukocyte Antigen (HLA) testing market is divided into two main types: molecular assay technologies and non-molecular assay technologies. Molecular assays, such as PCR and sequencing, analyze DNA to identify specific HLA genes and alleles, providing high sensitivity and specificity. Non-molecular assays, including serological techniques, rely on the detection of antibodies or proteins associated with HLA. Both types are crucial for organ transplantation, disease association studies, and personalized medicine, with distinct methodologies catering to various clinical needs.</p></p>
