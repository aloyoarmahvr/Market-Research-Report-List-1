<p><ul><li>One-Side Lamination Film</li><li>Double-Side Lamination Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soft touch lamination films market is segmented into two main types: one-side lamination film and double-side lamination film. One-side lamination film is used primarily to enhance the surface finish of printed materials on one side, providing a soft, tactile feel. In contrast, double-side lamination film applies a soft touch finish to both sides, offering enhanced protection and aesthetics. Both types are widely utilized in packaging, marketing materials, and luxury products to create an engaging tactile experience.</p></p>
