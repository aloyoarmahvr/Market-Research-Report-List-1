<p><p>The Aircraft Magneto Ignition System market comprises two main types: High-Tension and Low-Tension Ignition Systems. High-Tension Ignition Systems utilize a magneto to generate high-voltage electricity, firing spark plugs directly and ensuring reliable ignition in various conditions. In contrast, Low-Tension Ignition Systems operate at lower voltages and usually rely on battery support, making them less common but suitable for certain aircraft designs. Both systems play crucial roles in ensuring efficient engine performance and safety in aviation.</p></p>
