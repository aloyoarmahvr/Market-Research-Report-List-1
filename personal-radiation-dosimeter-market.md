<p><p>The personal radiation dosimeter market comprises various types, including Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLD), Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL) devices, and Radiophotoluminescent (RPL) dosimeters. TLDs measure ionizing radiation by heating crystals to release stored energy, while OSL dosimeters use light stimulation for measurement. RPL dosimeters are based on the luminescence properties of specific materials. Additionally, active dosimeters continuously monitor radiation levels and provide real-time data, enhancing safety in environments where exposure risks are prevalent. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on specific application needs.</p></p>
