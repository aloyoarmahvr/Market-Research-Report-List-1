<p><p>The breast imaging technology market comprises several types, including Molecular Breast Imaging (MBI), Positron Emission Tomography-Computed Tomography (PET-CT), and Positron Emission Mammography (PEM). MBI offers enhanced sensitivity for detecting tumors, utilizing a radiotracer for precise imaging. PET-CT combines metabolic imaging with conventional CT, aiding in cancer diagnosis and staging. PEM focuses on breast-specific imaging using a dedicated detection system. Other technologies encompass traditional mammography, MRI, and advancements such as 3D imaging, contributing to comprehensive breast cancer screening and diagnosis.</p></p>
