<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis</li><li>Vertical Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind turbine systems market comprises two primary types: horizontal axis wind turbines (HAWT) and vertical axis wind turbines (VAWT). HAWTs, characterized by their blades mounted on a horizontal rotor, are the most common due to their efficiency and higher energy output, particularly in large-scale applications. In contrast, VAWTs have blades that rotate around a vertical axis, offering advantages in urban environments, lower wind conditions, and easier maintenance. Both types cater to diverse applications, shaping the renewable energy landscape.</p></p>
