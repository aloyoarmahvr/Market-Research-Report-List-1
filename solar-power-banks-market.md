<p><p>The solar power bank market is categorized into two main types: single USB and dual USB. Single USB solar power banks typically feature one output port, making them suitable for charging a single device at a time, ideal for minimal users. In contrast, dual USB solar power banks offer two output ports, allowing simultaneous charging of multiple devices, catering to users with greater power needs or those traveling with more than one device. Both types harness solar energy for eco-friendly and portable charging solutions.</p></p>
