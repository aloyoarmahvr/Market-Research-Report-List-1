<p><p>The automotive air conditioning filter market includes primarily two types: particle filters and activated carbon filters. Particle filters focus on capturing airborne contaminants like dust, pollen, and soot, enhancing cabin air quality and ensuring a comfortable driving experience. Conversely, activated carbon filters are designed to absorb unpleasant odors and harmful gases, utilizing charcoal-based media to neutralize pollutants. Together, these filters significantly improve vehicle air quality, contributing to both passenger health and overall driving satisfaction.</p></p>
