<p><ul><li>Layered Green Roof Systems</li><li>Modular Green Roof Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Green roof systems are primarily categorized into layered and modular systems. Layered green roof systems involve multiple components, including waterproofing, drainage, growing medium, and vegetation, seamlessly integrated to support extensive or intensive plant growth. In contrast, modular green roof systems utilize pre-planted, interlocking trays or containers, allowing for easier installation and maintenance. Both types enhance urban ecology, improve energy efficiency, reduce stormwater runoff, and provide aesthetic benefits, catering to various architectural needs and environmental concerns in urban settings.</p></p>
