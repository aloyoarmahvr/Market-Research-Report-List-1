<p><strong>The Thermochromic Smart Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low E-Glasses</li><li>Active Glasses</li><li>Passive Glasses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermochromic smart glass market comprises three main types: Low E-glasses, Active glasses, and Passive glasses. Low E-glasses are designed to reflect infrared light, enhancing energy efficiency while regulating temperature. Active glasses change tint in response to environmental conditions, providing dynamic light control and privacy. Passive glasses, on the other hand, utilize inherent temperature-sensitive properties to alter color, allowing for a more static solution to solar heat gain. Each type contributes uniquely to energy conservation and user comfort in various applications.</p></p>
