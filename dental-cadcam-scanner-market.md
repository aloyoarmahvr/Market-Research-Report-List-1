<p><strong>The Dental CADCAM Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mouth-in</li><li>Desktop</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dental CAD/CAM scanner market is categorized into three main types: mouth-in, desktop, and others. Mouth-in scanners are portable devices used directly in the patient's mouth for intraoral scanning, providing real-time imaging and impressions. Desktop scanners are stationary units that digitize traditional dental impressions or models, offering high precision for laboratory use. The "other" category encompasses specialized scanners with unique functionalities, catering to specific dental applications, including orthodontics and prosthodontics, enhancing the versatility within the dental imaging market.</p></p>
