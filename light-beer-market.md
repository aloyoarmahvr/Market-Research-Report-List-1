<p><strong>The Light Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Limit Fermentation</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light beer market features two primary types based on their production methods: limit fermentation and dealcoholization. Limit fermentation involves controlling the fermentation process to produce lower-alcohol content beers while retaining flavor. In contrast, the dealcoholization method removes alcohol from fully fermented beer, resulting in a beverage that maintains the original taste profile with a significantly reduced alcohol percentage. Both methods cater to consumers seeking lighter options, emphasizing lower calories and enhanced drinkability without sacrificing quality.</p></p>
