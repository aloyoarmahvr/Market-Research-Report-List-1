<p><ul><li>Cement Ball Head</li><li>Press-Fit Ball Head</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hip replacement implants primarily feature two categories of ball heads: cement ball heads and press-fit ball heads. Cement ball heads are secured using a special bone cement, providing stability and immediate load-bearing capacity. In contrast, press-fit ball heads are designed to be implanted directly into the bone without cement, allowing for bone growth into the implant for natural stability over time. Other market types may include hybrid options or specialized designs tailored for specific patient needs, enhancing surgical outcomes.</p></p>
