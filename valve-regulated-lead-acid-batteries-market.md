<p><p>Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) batteries are a type of lead-acid battery that includes two main market types: Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize glass mats to absorb and immobilize the electrolyte, promoting efficient performance and fast recharge times, making them ideal for high-drain applications. In contrast, Gel batteries contain a silica-based gel electrolyte, offering enhanced deep cycle capabilities and better resistance to extreme temperatures. Both types are maintenance-free, making them popular in various industries.</p></p>
