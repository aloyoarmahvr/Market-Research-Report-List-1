<p><p>The Detacher EAS System market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) Systems** utilize electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects. </p><p>2. **Acoustic Magnetic (AM) Anti-Theft Systems** employ magnetic fields that trigger alarm systems when tags are removed improperly. </p><p>3. **Radio Frequency (RF) Systems** operate on radio waves, detecting unauthorized removal of tagged merchandise. </p><p>4. **Electromagnetic Wave (EM) Anti-Theft Systems** use electromagnetic fields to prevent theft. </p><p>5. **Other Markets** may include advanced combinations or new technologies for retail security.</p></p>
