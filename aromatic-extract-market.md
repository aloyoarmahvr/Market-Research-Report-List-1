<p><p>The aromatic extract market is primarily divided into two processes: Direct Chlorination (DC) and Hydrochlorination (HC). The DC process involves the direct introduction of chlorine to aromatic compounds, facilitating the production of chlorinated derivatives, which are utilized in various chemical applications. In contrast, the HC process involves the reaction of aromatic compounds with hydrogen chloride, resulting in a different set of chlorinated products. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs, including pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, and specialty chemicals.</p></p>
