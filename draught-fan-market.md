<p><p>The draught fan market consists of two main types: low-pressure fans and high-pressure fans. Low-pressure fans operate at lower static pressures, making them ideal for applications requiring large airflow volumes with minimal resistance, such as ventilation in buildings. In contrast, high-pressure fans generate greater static pressure, suitable for conveying air in more challenging systems, such as industrial processes and dust collection. Both types play crucial roles in various sectors, addressing different needs for airflow management and efficiency.</p></p>
