<p><p>Property management apps can be categorized into two main market types: on-cloud and on-premise. On-cloud apps are hosted on the service provider's servers, offering flexibility, accessibility, and reduced IT maintenance for users, as they can access the software from anywhere with an internet connection. On-premise apps, on the other hand, are installed locally on a company's servers, providing enhanced control over data security and customizability, but requiring more IT resources for maintenance and updates.</p></p>
